# Watch Industry Red Flags — Herkulio Intelligence

Red flags from real watch industry investigations. Use these to evaluate dealers.

## 🔴 CRITICAL — Walk Away Immediately

### Identity
- **OFAC/SDN match** — Sanctions list hit on target or associate
- **ICIJ Offshore Leaks** — Pandora Papers, Panama Papers as UBO/director
- **Active federal fraud case** — Wire fraud, money laundering, Ponzi
- **Interpol notice**
- **SEC/CFTC enforcement action**

### Transaction
- **Requests payment to third party** — Not the entity you're investigating
- **Wire to personal account** — Business deal, personal bank account
- **Refuses escrow** — For transactions >$10K
- **Pressure to close quickly** — "Someone else is interested"

## 🟠 HIGH RISK — Major Concerns

### Corporate
- **Entity < 12 months old** — Claims to be "established"
- **Multiple state registrations** — Mismatched principals across states
- **Privacy proxy registered agent** — Legit dealers use real addresses
- **No physical address** — Can't verify via Street View
- **Domain < 1 year old** — Claims established brand
- **WHOIS privacy** — For B2B dealer (suspicious)
- **Virtual office address** — Regus, WeWork as primary address

### Financial
- **No PPP loan record** — Claims high volume/payroll but no record
- **No property records** — In claimed location
- **Multiple civil judgments** — Unpaid debts
- **Prior bankruptcy** — Not disclosed

### Marketplace
- **Zero Chrono24** — Claims >$1M annual volume
- **Zero eBay history** — Claims retail operations
- **No BBB or complaints** — Unaccredited with negative reviews
- **Recently created social** — Instagram/Twitter < 6 months
- **Fake followers** — Bot engagement, <5% of claimed customer base
- **All testimonials fake** — From accounts with <100 followers

### Communication
- **VoIP/burner phone** — Not landline or real mobile
- **Gmail/Yahoo for B2B** — Not business domain email
- **No email domain** — Can't verify business email
- **Refuses video call** — For high-value transactions
- **Won't provide references** — From recent transactions

## 🟡 MEDIUM RISK — Dig Deeper

- Domain registered recently but claims long history
- Few social media posts but high follower count
- No negative reviews (can indicate fake presence)
- Single employee LLC claiming large operations
- Address is mailbox store (UPS Store, etc.)
- Phone number recently ported
- Business registered in different state than operations

## 🟢 GREEN FLAGS — Markers of Legitimacy

### Corporate
- ✅ Entity active (not dissolved) in registration state
- ✅ Physical address matches Street View
- ✅ Business email domain matches claimed domain
- ✅ Domain registered > 3 years
- ✅ Real landline or mobile (not VoIP)

### Marketplace
- ✅ Chrono24 presence consistent with claimed volume
- ✅ eBay history matches claimed operations
- ✅ BBB accredited or clean record
- ✅ Industry press mentions over multiple years

### Digital
- ✅ Instagram with organic engagement
- ✅ Real customer reviews (positive AND negative)
- ✅ Active social > 2 years
- ✅ Website professional, updated

### Legal/Financial
- ✅ No court records
- ✅ No adverse media
- ✅ Clean property records
- ✅ Consistent business history
